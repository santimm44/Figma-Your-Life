American Red Cross

Pride

I don't know why, but I am proud of being able to donate and to be able to donate as much as I have.

Now, the image above isn't the full story of the amount of times I have donated. It is specific to the platelets I have donated. I believe I have donated around 96 times. That's according to my phone's Donor American Red Cross App.

Donating NOT selling

I can't begin to say how many times I have been asked how much money I get for donating. That's so bizarre to me. I go to the American Red Cross to donate platelets (primarily), blood (occassionaly), and plasma (rarely). I do not expect to be copensated. Yet others often think I am making income while donating. I whole hearted believe if the day comes where I expect to receive compensation then I am no longer donating, but rather selling.

How often I go

I try going as often as I can. However, I recently hit the cap of blood I can safely lose in a given 356-day time frame. Now I need to wait a few weeks between visits.

Because I often donate blood in between platelets I find myself donating less platelets in the given year than what is possible. I think a person can donate platelets 21 to 24 times in a 365 day time frame. I am going to be taking a breaking from donating blood, so I can donate more platelets throughout the year.
