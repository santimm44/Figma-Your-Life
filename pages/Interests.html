Comics

Where to begin?

Comics are just so great. I don't remember the first comic I read, but I remember some of my first interactions was with newspaper comics. My family would receive weekly newspaper on Sundays and I would find the comics section and look at the pictures. I was too young to understand or read the comics, but I liked the cartoon pictures enough to just look at them.

I probably started reading comics when I was 15 or 16, if you don't consider manga a form of comic. If you do consider mangas as a form of comics then I began reading them around 12 years of age.

Lately I sparsely been able to read comics (thanks guys T-T). However, I try to read an issue or two every other weekend since starting CodeStack. Right now I am catching up on recent issues from the King Spawn and Spawn series by Todd McFarnlane.
